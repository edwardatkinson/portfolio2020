My Story In 3 Bullets

With the naive confidence that comes bundled with youth, I landed contracts with CEOs three times my age in the MD-VA-DC metro area. That was nervewracking. But I had fun, and the business grew.
  • You love music, right? Me too. So I went to university for a bachelor's and two graduate degrees, to get really good at it. I paid for these degrees up front by running my business remotely at the same time. I don't recommend that much sleep deprivation, but it'll work in a pinch.
